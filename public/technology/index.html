Lendable Technology

The Colonel By Library is pleased to make available to students lendable technology in the form of Chromebooks and iPad Mini tablets. Students can sign-out this technology for the day by going to the Library. Before students may borrow this technology from the Library, we ask that students, parents and/or legal guardians print and sign the CB Lendable Technology Agreement (click below) and return it to the Library.

All students are bound by the OCDSB Appropriate Use of Technology Agreement (you may review this school board policy by clicking below). We support and encourage the responsible use of technology by students through this policy document. This is to ensure that all technology is being used in a safe, legal, and responsible manner.

Any loss or damage of borrowed technology is the responsibility of the borrower. The Library will communicate directly with Parents and/or Legal Guardian for any such issues.
