Meet the Librarians

Mme. Tricia Leduc

Greetings CB Cougars, I am the Teacher-Librarian! Everyone is welcome to come discuss school work in the library, from research paths, to citing sources I will be happy to assist you when you stop by the library. Please check out our Twitter @colbylibrary and our Facebook page Colonel By Library! We will keep you updated with news about books, films and world events!

My teaching background is in the Canadian and World Studies Dept. with much of my focus being Geography, Civics, History and Law. As an immersion teacher know that I can help with any of your french work as well. I will be joining the IB teachers in coordinating and supervising Extended Essays, among other things. Do not be surprised when your class comes to the library - I will often be collaborating with your teachers on assignments and be teaching various lessons to you.
